以下示例：
配置pos="tr"定位右上角打标，相对父元素定位，注意设置父元素position: relative;
pos-offset设置微量偏移

HTML Code
{{!text1}}  
<div style="position: relative;">
    <mx-effects.icon pos="tr" type="error" content="8"/>
</div>

<div style="position: relative;">
    <mx-effects.icon pos="tr" type="error" content="99"/>
</div>

<div style="position: relative;">
    <mx-effects.icon pos="tr" type="error" content="999+"/>
</div>

<div style="position: relative;">
    <mx-effects.icon pos="tr" type="error" content="NEW"/>
</div>

<div style="position: relative;">
    <mx-effects.icon pos="tr" type="highlight" content="品牌色"/>
</div>

<div style="position: relative;">
    <mx-effects.icon pos="tr" type="dark" content="标签"/>
</div>

<div style="position: relative;">
    <mx-effects.icon pos="tr" mode="dot" type="error"/>
</div>

<div style="position: relative;">
    <i class="mc-iconfont fontsize-24">&#xe7ff;</i>
    <mx-effects.icon pos="tr" pos-offset="{{@{
        top: 2,
        left: -2
    }}}" mode="dot" type="error"/>
</div>
